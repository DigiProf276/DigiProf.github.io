DigiProf

Get help for school, wherever, whenever.

Getting and Providing Help Anytime

Due to Covid, many students who do not live in Vancouver and cannot attend office hours, or TA Hours. Students in Math Courses in particular struggle to get help from TA’s and Professors, because sometimes an email is not enough to help a student learn the concepts. And sometimes discussion boards are not enough to answer a student’s question in the depth that it needs to be answered.

Asynronous Video Conferencing Done Right

DigiProf helps students and teachers learn asynchronously by sending video questions and answers, to walk through the process of solving questions. Each student sends a short video outlining their attempt at a question, and where they are struggling. The teacher can see the video at their own time, and can make a video reply, outlining the steps and how to complete the problem. It will also allow the professor to answer commonly asked questions. This places all students across different time zones on the same playing field.
